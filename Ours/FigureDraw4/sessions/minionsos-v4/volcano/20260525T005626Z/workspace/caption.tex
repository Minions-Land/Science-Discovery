\caption{Volcano plot of differential expression across 3{,}000 genes.
Each point is one gene; x-axis shows $\log_2$ fold change and y-axis shows
$-\log_{10}(p\text{-value})$.
\textbf{235 genes are significantly up-regulated and 230 are down-regulated}
(thresholds: $|\log_2\text{FC}| \geq 1.0$, $-\log_{10}p \geq 2.0$).
Red: up-regulated; blue: down-regulated; grey: not significant (dashed lines
mark thresholds).
The five genes with the highest $-\log_{10}p$ among significant hits are
labelled.
$n = 3{,}000$ genes; gene identifiers are index-based (no gene-name metadata
provided in data.json).}
