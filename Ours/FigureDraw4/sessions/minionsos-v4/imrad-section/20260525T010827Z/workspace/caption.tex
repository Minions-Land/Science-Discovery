\caption{Methods section for a prospective three-centre cohort study of non-invasive fibrosis-score prediction in \textsc{MASLD} patients (\textit{Cell Reports Medicine}; STROBE items 4--12 covered). The analytic cohort comprised \textbf{1{,}987 participants} (baseline $n = 2{,}412$; 17.6\% lost to follow-up) followed for a median of 3.4 years (IQR 2.6--4.1). Primary analysis: Cox proportional-hazards regression with time-varying covariates, adjusted for age, sex, BMI, diabetes, alcohol intake, and baseline fibrosis stage; missing data handled by multiple imputation ($m = 10$; \texttt{mice~3.16.0}); sensitivity analyses included E-value for unmeasured confounding and inverse-probability-weighted analysis for healthy-volunteer bias.}
