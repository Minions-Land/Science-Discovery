\caption{Forest plot of 12 studies reporting the effect of intervention vs.\ control on a binary outcome. Each row shows the point estimate (filled square, area proportional to inverse-variance weight) and 95\% confidence interval. The diamond at the bottom represents the fixed-effects pooled estimate computed by inverse-variance weighting (\textbf{pooled log OR = 0.245, 95\% CI [0.196, 0.294]}), indicating a statistically significant positive effect of the intervention. The dashed vertical line marks the null (log OR = 0). Error bars: 95\% CI ($\pm 1.96 \times \mathrm{SE}$). $N = 12$ studies.}
