\caption{\textbf{Training-objective derivation for \textsc{RetroDiff}.}
\textbf{(Eq.~\ref{eq:elbo})}~The evidence lower bound (ELBO) lower-bounds the
log-likelihood $\log p_\theta(x)$ via Jensen's inequality applied to the concave $\log$.
\textbf{(Eq.~\ref{eq:decomp})}~Markov factorisation of the diffusion chain decomposes the
ELBO into a reconstruction term at $t\!=\!1$ and a sum of per-step KL divergences between
the forward posterior and the learned reverse kernel; each KL reduces to a weighted
$\ell_2$ denoising loss in closed form.
\textbf{(Eq.~\ref{eq:retro})}~\textsc{RetroDiff}'s contribution: conditioning the ELBO on
the retrieved exemplar set $R(x)$ propagates nearest-neighbour context into every
denoising step of the reverse chain, giving the final per-sample objective whose
expectation over $p_{\mathrm{data}}$ defines the \textsc{RetroDiff} training loss
$\mathcal{L}_{\mathrm{RetroDiff}}(\theta)$.}
