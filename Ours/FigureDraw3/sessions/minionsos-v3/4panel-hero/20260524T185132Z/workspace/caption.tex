  \textbf{Four-panel overview of the proposed method.}
  %
  (\textbf{A})~Method overview: a three-stage pipeline comprising
  Input~Encoding (self-attention and positional embedding),
  Attentive~Memory (episodic memory cache), and
  Adaptive~Decoding (residual skill adapter), connected by
  feed-forward passes from Input to Output.
  %
  (\textbf{B})~Ablation study on five variants;
  removing the attention module (\texttt{-attn}) causes the largest
  drop (\textbf{$-$6.5 pp} from 64.7\% to 58.2\%), confirming
  attention as the most critical component.
  Bars show per-variant accuracy (\%); full model highlighted in blue.
  %
  (\textbf{C})~Training loss curves over 30k steps (log-scale);
  Ours (blue) converges faster and reaches a \textbf{final loss
  \(\mathbf{0.36}\) vs.\ 0.19 lower than Baseline~(0.36)},
  annotated with a double-headed arrow at the terminal step.
  %
  (\textbf{D})~5\(\times\)5 sensitivity sweep over five
  sensitivity parameters (S0--S4) and five probe depths (P0--P4);
  cell values are scores in $[0,1]$; high-scoring cells ($>$0.85)
  are bold-annotated.
  %
  Error bars and confidence intervals are not shown (single-run data
  from \texttt{data.json}); statistical uncertainty should be
  reported in future replicated experiments.
