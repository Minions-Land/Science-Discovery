\caption{Volcano plot of 3{,}000 genes showing differential expression ($-\!\log_{10}p$ vs.\ $\log_2$ fold change). Upregulated genes (red; $n=235$) satisfy $\log_2\text{FC}>1$ and $-\!\log_{10}p>2$; downregulated genes (blue; $n=230$) satisfy $\log_2\text{FC}<-1$ and $-\!\log_{10}p>2$; all remaining genes are non-significant (grey; $n=2535$). Dashed lines mark the thresholds. The five most significant genes (by $-\!\log_{10}p$) among significant genes are annotated. Gene labels are positional indices from the input data (no gene-name field was provided).}
