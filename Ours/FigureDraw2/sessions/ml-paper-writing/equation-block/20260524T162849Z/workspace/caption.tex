\caption{%
  \textbf{RetroDiff training objective derivation.}
  \textbf{(1)}~The evidence lower bound (ELBO) on $\log p_\theta(x)$,
  obtained by Jensen's inequality applied to the diffusion joint
  $p_\theta(x_{0:T})$.
  \textbf{(2)}~Canonical decomposition of the ELBO into a
  reconstruction term and a sum of per-step KL divergences between the
  tractable forward posterior $q(x_{t-1}|x_t,x)$ and the learned
  reverse step $p_\theta(x_{t-1}|x_t)$.
  \textbf{(3)}~RetroDiff's retrieval-augmented objective: the ELBO is
  conditioned on a set $R(x)$ of $k$ exemplars retrieved for each
  training sample $x$, and the result is averaged over the data
  distribution $p_{\mathrm{data}}$.  Setting $R(x)=\varnothing$
  recovers the standard diffusion objective.%
