\caption{Volcano plot of differential gene expression for $n=3{,}000$ genes.
Each point represents one gene plotted by $\log_2$ fold change (x-axis) versus
$-\log_{10}(p\text{-value})$ (y-axis).
Dashed lines mark the significance thresholds ($|\!\log_2\mathrm{FC}|\geq1$,
$-\log_{10}p\geq2$; i.e.\ $p\leq0.01$).
Upregulated genes (right, \textcolor[HTML]{DD4949}{red}, $n=235$) and
downregulated genes (left, \textcolor[HTML]{4C72B0}{blue}, $n=230$) exceed both thresholds;
non-significant genes are shown in grey ($n=2{,}535$).
The five most significant genes (highest $-\log_{10}p$ among the regulated set) are annotated by index.
Gene identifiers are not provided in the source data; labels reflect positional indices.}
