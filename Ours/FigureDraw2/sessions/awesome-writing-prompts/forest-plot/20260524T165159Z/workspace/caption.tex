\caption{Forest plot of 12 studies reporting the effect of an intervention on a binary outcome, expressed as log odds ratios with 95\,\% confidence intervals (CI). Square markers are sized proportionally to inverse-variance weight. The pooled fixed-effects estimate (inverse-variance method) is shown as a red diamond: OR\,=\,0.245 (95\,\% CI [0.196,\,0.294]). The dashed vertical line indicates no effect (log OR\,=\,0). Pooled estimate was computed from the provided point estimates and standard errors; no heterogeneity statistic ($I^2$) was available from the supplied data.}
