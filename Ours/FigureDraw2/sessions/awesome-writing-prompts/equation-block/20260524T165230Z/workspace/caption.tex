  \textbf{RetroDiff training objective derivation.}
  Equation~\eqref{eq:elbo} is the standard variational lower bound (ELBO) on the
  log-likelihood, obtained by Jensen's inequality.
  Equation~\eqref{eq:decomp} decomposes the ELBO into a reconstruction term and
  a sum of per-step KL divergences, each of which is tractable because the forward
  posterior $q(x_{t-1}|x_t,x)$ is Gaussian.
  Equation~\eqref{eq:retro} is RetroDiff's contribution: the per-sample objective
  is conditioned on a set $R(x)$ of $k$ exemplars retrieved from the training corpus,
  and the expectation is taken over the data distribution.
  Setting $R(x)=\varnothing$ recovers the standard DDPM objective.%
