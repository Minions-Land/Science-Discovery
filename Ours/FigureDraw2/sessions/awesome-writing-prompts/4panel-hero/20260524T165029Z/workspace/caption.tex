\caption{\textbf{Four-panel hero figure.}

\textbf{(B)}~Ablation study: removing attention (\texttt{-attn}), memory (\texttt{-mem}),

reduces accuracy relative to the full model (64.7\%).
\textbf{(C)}~Training loss curves over 30\,k steps; our method (solid blue) converges
faster and to a lower final loss than the baseline (dashed grey).
\textbf{(D)}~Sensitivity sweep across a $5\times5$ grid of hyperparameters $S$ and $P$;
cell values indicate normalised performance scores (0--1).}
