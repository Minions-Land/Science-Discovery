\caption{Volcano plot of 3{,}000 genes showing $-\log_{10}(p)$ versus $\log_2$ fold change.
Dashed lines mark significance thresholds ($|\log_2\text{FC}|>1.0$; $-\log_{10}p>2.0$).
Upregulated genes (red, $n$ right of threshold) and downregulated genes (blue, $n$ left of threshold) are highlighted relative to non-significant genes (grey).
The five most significant genes (highest $-\log_{10}p$) are labelled.}
