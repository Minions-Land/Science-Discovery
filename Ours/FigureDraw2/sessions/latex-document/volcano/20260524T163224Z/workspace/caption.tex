\caption{Volcano plot of differential gene expression across $n=3{,}000$ genes.
The horizontal axis shows $\log_2$ fold change and the vertical axis shows $-\log_{10}$ adjusted $p$-value.
Genes with $|\log_2\text{FC}|>1$ and $-\log_{10}p>2$ (dashed lines) are coloured red (up-regulated, $n=235$) or blue (down-regulated, $n=230$); remaining genes are shown in grey ($n=2{,}535$).
The five most significant genes (by $-\log_{10}p$) are labelled.}
