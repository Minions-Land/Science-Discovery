\caption{Relative cell viability across four treatment conditions ($n=100$ per group).
Violins show the kernel-density estimate; boxes span the interquartile range with whiskers
extending to 1.5$\times$IQR; white diamonds mark medians; semi-transparent points are
individual observations (jittered, $\alpha=0.35$).
Significance brackets compare each condition to Control by two-sided Mann--Whitney $U$ test
(*** $p<0.001$, ** $p<0.01$, * $p<0.05$, ns $p\geq0.05$).
Dashed line at viability $= 1.0$ indicates the untreated reference level.}
