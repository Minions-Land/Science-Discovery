  \textbf{RetroDiff training objective derivation.}
  Equation~\eqref{eq:elbo} is the standard variational lower bound (ELBO) on $\log p_\theta(x)$,
  obtained by Jensen's inequality.
  Equation~\eqref{eq:decomp} decomposes the ELBO into a reconstruction term and a sum of
  per-step KL divergences between the tractable forward posterior $q(x_{t-1}|x_t,x)$ and the
  learned reverse step $p_\theta(x_{t-1}|x_t)$.
  Equation~\eqref{eq:retro} is RetroDiff's contribution: the per-sample ELBO is conditioned on
  a set $R(x)$ of $k$ exemplars retrieved from the training corpus, and the objective averages
  this retrieval-augmented bound over the data distribution $p_{\mathrm{data}}$.
