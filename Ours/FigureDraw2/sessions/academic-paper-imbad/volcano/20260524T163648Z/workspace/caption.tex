\caption{Volcano plot of differential expression for 3{,}000 genes. The $x$-axis shows $\log_2$ fold change and the $y$-axis shows $-\log_{10}(p)$. Genes exceeding both thresholds ($|\log_2\text{FC}|>1$ and $-\log_{10}(p)>2$) are coloured red (up-regulated, $n=235$) or blue (down-regulated, $n=230$); all remaining genes are grey ($n=2{,}535$).
