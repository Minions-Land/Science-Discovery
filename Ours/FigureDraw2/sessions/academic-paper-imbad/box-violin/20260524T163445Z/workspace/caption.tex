\caption{Relative cell viability across four treatment conditions ($n=100$ per group).
Violin plots show the kernel-density estimate of the distribution; overlaid box plots
depict the interquartile range and median (white line); individual observations are
shown as jittered semi-transparent points ($\alpha=0.35$).
The dashed horizontal line marks viability~$=1$ (untreated reference).
Significance brackets indicate pairwise Mann-Whitney $U$ tests versus Control
(*** $p<0.001$, ** $p<0.01$, * $p<0.05$, ns $p\geq0.05$).
Conditions are ordered Control $\to$ Drug-A 10\,µM $\to$ Drug-A 50\,µM $\to$ Combo.}
