\caption{Volcano plot of differential expression across 3{,}000 genes. Each point represents one gene; the $x$-axis shows $\log_2$ fold change and the $y$-axis shows $-\log_{10}(p)$. Dashed lines mark the significance thresholds ($|\log_2\text{FC}| \geq 1$, $-\log_{10}(p) \geq 2$). Upregulated genes (red, $n=235$) and downregulated genes (blue, $n=230$) are highlighted; the remaining 2{,}535 genes are non-significant (grey). The five most significant genes (by $-\log_{10}(p)$) are labelled. Gene identifiers are index-based (no external annotation provided in the input data).}
