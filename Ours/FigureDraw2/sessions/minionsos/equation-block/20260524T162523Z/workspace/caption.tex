\caption{Training objective derivation for \textbf{RetroDiff}.
Equation~\eqref{eq:elbo} is the standard variational lower bound (ELBO) on the log-likelihood,
obtained by Jensen's inequality.
Equation~\eqref{eq:decomp} decomposes the ELBO into a reconstruction term and a sum of
per-step KL divergences between the forward posterior and the learned reverse transitions.
Equation~\eqref{eq:retro} is RetroDiff's contribution: the per-sample ELBO is conditioned on
a set $R(x)$ of $k$ exemplars retrieved from the training corpus, and the final objective
averages this conditioned bound over the data distribution.}
