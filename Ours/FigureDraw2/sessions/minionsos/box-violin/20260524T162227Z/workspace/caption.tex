\caption{Relative cell viability across four treatment conditions ($n = 100$ per group).
Violin shapes show kernel-density estimates; overlaid box plots indicate the interquartile
range, with the median highlighted by a white circle.
Individual observations are shown as jittered points ($\alpha = 0.35$).
Significance brackets reflect two-sided Mann-Whitney $U$ tests vs.\ Control
(*** $p < 0.001$, ** $p < 0.01$, * $p < 0.05$, ns $p \geq 0.05$).
Drug-A reduces viability in a dose-dependent manner, with the combination condition
(Drug-A 50\,\textmu M + partner drug) producing the greatest suppression.}
