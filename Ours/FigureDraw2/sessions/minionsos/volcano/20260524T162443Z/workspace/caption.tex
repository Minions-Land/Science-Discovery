\caption{Volcano plot of 3{,}000 genes. Each point represents one gene; horizontal position shows log$_2$ fold change and vertical position shows $-$log$_{10}$(p-value). Dashed lines mark the significance thresholds ($|\text{log}_2\text{FC}| = 1$; $-\text{log}_{10}(p) = 2$, corresponding to $p = 0.01$). Red points (n\,=\,235) are significantly upregulated; blue points (n\,=\,230) are significantly downregulated; grey points (n\,=\,2{,}535) are non-significant. The five genes with the highest $-$log$_{10}$(p) among significant genes are labelled.}
