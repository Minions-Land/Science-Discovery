\caption{Volcano plot of differential expression for 3{,}000 genes.
Each point represents one gene; the $x$-axis shows $\log_2$ fold change and the $y$-axis shows $-\log_{10}(p)$.
Genes with $|\log_2\text{FC}|>1$ and $-\log_{10}(p)>2$ are coloured red (up-regulated, $n=235$) or blue (down-regulated, $n=230$); all other genes are grey ($n=2{,}535$).
Dashed lines mark the significance thresholds.
The five genes with the highest $-\log_{10}(p)$ among significant genes are labelled.
Gene labels are generic identifiers (Gene\textit{N}) because no gene-name field was provided in the data.}
