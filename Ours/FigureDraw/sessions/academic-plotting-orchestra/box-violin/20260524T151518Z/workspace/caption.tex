\caption{Relative cell viability across four treatment conditions ($n=100$ per group).
Violin plots show kernel-density estimates; overlaid box plots indicate median (centre line),
interquartile range (box), and 1.5$\times$IQR whiskers. Jittered points ($\alpha=0.35$)
represent individual observations. Significance brackets compare each condition to Control
by two-sided Mann--Whitney $U$ test: $^{*}p<0.05$; $^{**}p<0.01$; $^{***}p<0.001$; ns,
not significant. Drug-A 50~\textmu M and Combo conditions show strong, significant reductions
in viability relative to Control.}
