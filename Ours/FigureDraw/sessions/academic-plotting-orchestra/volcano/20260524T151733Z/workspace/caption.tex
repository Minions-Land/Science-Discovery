\caption{Volcano plot of differential expression across $n=3{,}000$ genes. Each point represents one gene, coloured by significance status: upregulated (red; $\log_2\text{FC} > 1$, $-\log_{10}p > 2$, $n=$ up), downregulated (blue; $\log_2\text{FC} < -1$, $-\log_{10}p > 2$), or not significant (grey). Dashed lines indicate the fold-change ($|\log_2\text{FC}|=1$) and significance ($-\log_{10}p=2$, corresponding to $p=0.01$) thresholds. The five genes with the highest statistical significance are labelled by index. Gene labels are index-based; no gene-name metadata was provided in the source data.}
