\caption{\textbf{RetroDiff architecture.} Boxes-and-arrows diagram of the RetroDiff pipeline. A user \textit{Query} is passed to the \textit{Retriever}, which fetches relevant entries from the knowledge base (\textit{KB}); retrieved context is forwarded to the \textit{Diffusion Decoder}. The \textit{Prior Network} receives a control signal from the decoder (dashed arrow) to condition the generative process. The decoder produces the final \textit{Generated Sample}. Shaded regions denote the \textit{Retrieval} and \textit{Generation} subsystems. Solid arrows indicate data flow; dashed arrows indicate control/feedback.}
