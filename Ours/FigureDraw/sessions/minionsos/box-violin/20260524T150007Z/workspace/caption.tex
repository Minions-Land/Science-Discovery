\caption{Relative cell viability across four treatment conditions ($n=100$ per group).
Violin shapes show the kernel-density estimate of the full distribution; overlaid box plots show the interquartile range and median (black bar); individual observations are shown as jittered points ($\alpha=0.35$).
Significance brackets indicate Mann--Whitney $U$ tests (two-sided) against the Control group: $^{***}p<0.001$, $^{**}p<0.01$, $^{*}p<0.05$, ns~$p\geq0.05$.
Drug-A reduces viability in a dose-dependent manner, and the combination treatment (Combo) produces the greatest suppression.}
