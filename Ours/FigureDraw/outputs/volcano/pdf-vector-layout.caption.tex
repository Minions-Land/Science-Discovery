\caption{Volcano plot of 3{,}000 genes. Each point represents one gene plotted as $\log_2$ fold change (x-axis) versus $-\log_{10}(p)$ (y-axis). Genes are coloured by significance category: up-regulated (red; $\log_2\text{FC} \geq 1$, $-\log_{10}p \geq 2$), down-regulated (blue; $\log_2\text{FC} \leq -1$, $-\log_{10}p \geq 2$), and non-significant (grey). Dashed lines mark the fold-change and significance thresholds. The five genes with the highest $-\log_{10}(p)$ values are labelled by index.}
