\caption{Volcano plot of differential expression across 3{,}000 genes. Each point represents one gene; the $x$-axis shows $\log_2$ fold change and the $y$-axis shows $-\log_{10}(p\text{-value})$. Genes are coloured by significance category: up-regulated (red; $\log_2\text{FC} > 1$, $-\log_{10}p > 2$), down-regulated (blue; $\log_2\text{FC} < -1$, $-\log_{10}p > 2$), and non-significant (grey). Dashed lines mark the fold-change ($|\log_2\text{FC}| = 1$) and significance ($-\log_{10}p = 2$) thresholds. The five genes with the highest $-\log_{10}p$ values are labelled by index.}
