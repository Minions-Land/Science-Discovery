\caption{Volcano plot of 3{,}000 genes. Horizontal axis: $\log_2$ fold change; vertical axis: $-\log_{10}(p\text{-value})$. Dashed lines mark the significance thresholds ($|\log_2\text{FC}|=1$; $-\log_{10}p=2$). Upregulated genes (red, $n=235$) satisfy $\log_2\text{FC}>1$ and $-\log_{10}p>2$; downregulated genes (blue, $n=230$) satisfy $\log_2\text{FC}<-1$ and $-\log_{10}p>2$; all remaining genes are non-significant (grey, $n=2{,}535$). The five most significant genes (highest $-\log_{10}p$ among significant calls) are labelled by index.}
