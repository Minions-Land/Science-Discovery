**规则**：Future Work 必须具体。

**要求**：1–2 个具体方向，不要空话。

**正确示例**：
> "We plan to extend RetroDiff to multi-chain complexes by conditioning on inter-chain distance matrices."

**错误示例**：
> "This work opens exciting new avenues for future research."（空话）
> "We hope our method inspires further investigation."（空话）
