## Polished

Current protein language model (PLM) training depends on large sequence datasets with uneven species coverage and imbalanced functional annotation. These biases can limit generalisation to rare protein families and under-characterised functional classes. Here, we introduce a training framework designed to improve PLM robustness for long-tail protein families. Across six benchmarks, the framework outperformed existing baselines, although the size of these gains should be reported in the Results.
