## Polished

Treated cells showed a 3.2-fold increase in target-gene expression compared with controls (Fig. 2c; n = 4 per group; two-tailed t-test, p = 0.011). Single-cell RNA sequencing of the same population detected a rare subset, representing approximately 4% of cells, that co-expressed the target gene and a known co-activator. This subset was enriched after treatment (1.8-fold; hypergeometric p = 0.003). Co-activator knockdown abolished the response, linking the co-activator to the measured expression change.
