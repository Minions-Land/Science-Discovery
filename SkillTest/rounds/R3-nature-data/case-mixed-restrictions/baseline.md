## Data Availability

The ECG recordings generated in this study for 482 patients from three German hospitals will be deposited in Zenodo under restricted access, consistent with patient privacy and the DZHK consortium ethics approval Heidelberg-2024-127. The RNA-seq data from cardiac tissue biopsies of 38 patients are not publicly available because patient consent restricts redistribution; access may be requested from the corresponding author and will be considered subject to institutional approval. The study also reanalysed public data from UK Biobank under application 78922 and PhysioNet's MIMIC-IV-ECG database; these datasets are available from their original repositories subject to their access procedures and citation requirements.

## Code Availability

The preprocessing code and deep-learning model weights generated by the authors will be made available in a public code repository before publication. The custom annotation pipeline is available except for the internally developed peak-detection model, which is patent pending and will not be released at submission. Requests for additional implementation details may be directed to the corresponding author.
