## Polished

Single-cell RNA-seq has revealed rare cell populations at high resolution. Vento-Tormo et al. (2018) profiled the maternal-fetal interface and identified a previously uncharacterised decidual-NK subset, showing that immune cell heterogeneity in this niche far exceeds what bulk transcriptomics suggested. We applied similar high-resolution mapping to the spleen, where conventional dendritic cells exhibit a distinct transcriptional signature during chronic infection. We sequenced 12,400 splenic dendritic cells from C57BL/6 mice across three infection timepoints. Differential expression analysis identified 47 genes upregulated specifically in the late-infection cluster, including markers of metabolic stress. This subset may represent a functionally distinct state adapted to persistent antigenic challenge.
