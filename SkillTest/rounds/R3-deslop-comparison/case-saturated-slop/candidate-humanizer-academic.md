## Polished

Attention mechanisms are central to transformer architectures because they enable models to assign higher weight to relevant parts of an input sequence. In "Attention Is All You Need", Vaswani et al. (2017) introduced an attention-based architecture that changed sequence modelling and reduced reliance on recurrent computation. The approach has scaled to applications in natural language processing, machine translation, and protein structure prediction. Recent analyses of attention patterns have provided evidence about how transformer models process information. We build on this evidence by introducing an attention variant that may support broader use of attention-based architectures.
