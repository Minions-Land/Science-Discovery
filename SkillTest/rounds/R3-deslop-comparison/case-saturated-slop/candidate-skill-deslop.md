## Polished

Transformers use attention mechanisms to weight the parts of an input sequence that matter for a given prediction. Vaswani et al. (2017) established this design in "Attention Is All You Need", changing sequence modelling by replacing recurrence-heavy architectures with attention-based computation. The same mechanism has scaled to tasks beyond natural language processing, including machine translation and protein structure prediction. Recent studies of attention patterns have also clarified how transformer models process information. We build on this work with a new attention variant designed to extend the range of attention-based architectures.
