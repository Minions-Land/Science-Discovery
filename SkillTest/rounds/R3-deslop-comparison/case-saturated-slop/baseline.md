## Polished

Attention mechanisms are central to modern transformer architectures because they let models weight the most relevant parts of an input sequence. Vaswani et al. (2017) introduced this approach in "Attention Is All You Need", changing how researchers design models for sequence modelling. The mechanism's simplicity and scalability have supported applications beyond natural language processing, including machine translation and protein structure prediction. Recent work has also examined attention patterns to understand how transformer models process information. Building on this literature, our work introduces a new attention variant that may broaden the use of attention-based architectures.
