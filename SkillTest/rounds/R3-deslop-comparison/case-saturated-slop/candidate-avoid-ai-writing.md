## Polished

Attention mechanisms are a core component of transformer architectures because they allow models to focus on relevant positions in an input sequence. Vaswani et al. (2017) introduced the architecture in "Attention Is All You Need", which changed sequence modelling by making attention the main computational operation. Its simple design and ability to scale have supported applications in machine translation and protein structure prediction. More recent work has analyzed attention patterns to study how transformers process information. We extend this line of work with a new attention variant that may be useful for future transformer architectures.
