## Polished

Researchers use attention mechanisms in transformer architectures to let models weight relevant parts of an input sequence. Vaswani et al. (2017) introduced this design in "Attention Is All You Need", which changed sequence modelling by making attention the main operation. The design scales across tasks, including machine translation and protein structure prediction. Recent studies of attention patterns help explain how transformer models process information. We build on that work with a new attention variant for attention-based architectures.
