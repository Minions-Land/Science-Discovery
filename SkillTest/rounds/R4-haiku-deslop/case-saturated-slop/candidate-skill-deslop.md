## Polished

Attention mechanisms enable transformers to selectively process input sequences, a capability that has become central to modern NLP. Vaswani et al. (2017) formalized this insight in "Attention Is All You Need," establishing the attention-based architecture as the foundation for sequence modeling. The mechanism's simplicity and scalability have enabled applications across machine translation, protein folding, and beyond. Recent work has illuminated how attention patterns encode linguistic and structural information. This paper introduces a novel attention variant designed to improve upon existing approaches. We evaluate its performance across standard benchmarks and analyze its learned representations.
