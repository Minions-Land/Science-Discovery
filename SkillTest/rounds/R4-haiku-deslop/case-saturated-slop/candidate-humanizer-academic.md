## Polished

Attention mechanisms are central to modern transformer architectures. Vaswani et al. (2017) introduced the attention mechanism in "Attention Is All You Need," enabling models to selectively focus on relevant parts of input sequences. This approach has proven effective across natural language processing tasks and beyond, including machine translation and protein structure prediction. Recent work has characterized how attention patterns encode different linguistic and structural phenomena. We build on this foundation by proposing a novel attention variant that addresses specific limitations in existing approaches. Our analysis demonstrates the effectiveness of this variant across standard benchmarks and suggests directions for further refinement.
