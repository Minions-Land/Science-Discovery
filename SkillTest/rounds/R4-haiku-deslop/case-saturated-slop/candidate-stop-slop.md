## Polished

Attention mechanisms enable transformers to focus on salient input features, making them central to modern sequence modelling. Vaswani et al. (2017) introduced this architecture in "Attention Is All You Need," demonstrating that attention alone—without recurrence or convolution—could match or exceed prior approaches. The mechanism's simplicity and scalability have enabled applications across machine translation, question answering, and protein structure prediction. Recent work has characterized attention dynamics, revealing how these models allocate representational capacity across input positions. This paper builds on these foundations by introducing a novel attention variant designed to improve both efficiency and interpretability. We evaluate the variant across standard benchmarks and analyze its learned attention patterns to validate the proposed improvements.
