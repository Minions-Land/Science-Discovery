## Polished

Attention mechanisms have become central to modern sequence modelling. Vaswani et al. (2017) demonstrated in "Attention Is All You Need" that attention alone suffices for effective sequence transduction, eliminating the need for recurrence. The mechanism's simplicity and scalability have enabled widespread adoption across diverse domains—machine translation, language understanding, and protein structure prediction among them. Recent work has illuminated the interpretability of attention patterns, revealing how these models allocate representational capacity across input sequences. This paper contributes a novel attention variant designed to address specific limitations in existing approaches, with implications for both efficiency and model expressiveness.
