## Polished

Attention mechanisms are central to modern transformer architectures. Vaswani et al. (2017) introduced the transformer in "Attention Is All You Need," establishing a new foundation for sequence modelling. The mechanism's core insight—that models can learn to weight different input positions—proved effective across natural language processing, machine translation, and protein structure prediction. Recent work has characterized how attention patterns encode linguistic and structural information. This paper introduces a novel attention variant designed to improve upon existing approaches. We evaluate its performance across standard benchmarks and analyze its learned representations.
